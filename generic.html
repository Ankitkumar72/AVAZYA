AVAZAYA is a spectacular tech fest organized by CMREC (CMR Engineering College) that promises to be an unforgettable experience for tech enthusiasts from all over the country. This One-day event is a perfect platform for students to showcase their technical prowess, creativity, and innovation in various fields of engineering.

The fest features a diverse range of competitions, workshops, and events that cater to a broad spectrum of interests. Some of the exciting events include coding competitions, hardware design competitions, robotics competitions, and a hackathon, which brings together some of the best minds in the industry to solve real-world problems.

The fest also provides an opportunity for students to network and interact with peers.Students can showcase their projects, prototypes, and ideas and receive valuable feedback from experts in the field.

AVAZAYA is not just limited to tech enthusiasts; it also has a fun and entertaining side with cultural events, musical performances, and food stalls. The event aims to provide a holistic experience that caters to the intellectual, social, and cultural aspects of a student's life.

In summary, AVAZAYA is a comprehensive tech fest that offers a perfect blend of technical challenges, industry insights, and cultural experiences. It is an ideal platform for students to hone their technical skills, learn from experts in the industry, and showcase their talents on a national level.
