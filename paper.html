Paper Presentation

Paper Presentation is an event where the participants are required to present on any topic of interest, in papers. The event be organized as part of a seminar oor as a conference event focused specifically on paper presentation.
The teams are required to make pages: Minimum - 3 Maximum - 5

During the presentation, the participant will have a limited amount of time, typically between 15 to 30 minutes, to present their research to the audience. They may use visual aids such as slides, charts, or graphs to help convey their message. After the presentation, there may be a question and answer session, during which the audience can ask the presenter questions about their research.
The goal of a paper presentation event is to provide a platform for researchers to share their findings, ideas, and insights with a broader audience, and to receive feedback and suggestions from peers and experts in their field. These events can be valuable opportunities for participants to gain exposure, build networks, and receive recognition for their work.

Template: IEEE - Manuscript Templates for Conference Proceedings
Topic: All technical or CSE domain topics.
Rules:
• One hard copy and one softcopy(pdf).
• Explanation should be done with a ppt presentation.
• 7 min minimum presentation and 3 min Q & A.
• Team size must be 1-3 members.
• Thorough knowledge on the topic is required for the Q & A session.
• Jury’s decision is based on the various aspects and is final.

Registration fee: Free.
