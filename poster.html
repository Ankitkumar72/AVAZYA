poster Presentation

The poster presentation event at our techfest is an exciting opportunity for students to showcase their research and projects in a visually engaging format. Participants will design a poster that highlights their work in technology and engineering, using images, graphs, and diagrams to communicate their findings.

During the event, participants will have the chance to present their poster to a panel of judges and receive feedback on their work. This is a great opportunity for students to improve their presentation skills and receive valuable insights from experts in the field.The poster presentation event is open to students from various disciplines, including computer science, electronics, and mechanical engineering. It's a chance for participants to share their innovative ideas and connect with like-minded individuals in the tech community.

Overall, the poster presentation event is an exciting way for students to showcase their work, improve their communication skills, and connect with industry experts. We're looking forward to seeing the creativity and innovation on display at this year's techfest!
